FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes. Upgrade anytime from your dashboard. Downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
Q
How is IRDAI license verified?
In Phase 1, our admin team manually verifies your IRDAI license number within 24 hours. Phase 2 will automate this via API.
Q
What payment methods are accepted?
All payments are processed via Razorpay — UPI, debit/credit card, net banking, and wallets are accepted.
Q
Are testimonials really OTP-verified?
Yes. Every testimonial on YVITY requires the reviewer to verify their mobile number via OTP. No fake reviews.
Q
What is 'Recommend this Professional'?
Gold-only feature. Logged-in users can formally recommend a Gold advisor. Recommendation count appears on the profile header.
Q
Can I share my profile without login?
Yes. Your profile link can be shared via WhatsApp, visiting card QR code, or directly. Viewers don't need an account to view your profile.
